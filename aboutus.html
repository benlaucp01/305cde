Drug Abuse problem is social issue that happen in different countries and affecting many families. The harm bring from drug abuse are irreversible. We think that the best way to stop is to prevent, we want to let more people understand the consquence drug abuse can cause. Public who have drug abuse are usually under-estimated the consquence as they do not have enough knowledge about drugs and the effect of abuse.

We are organization aim at providing Drug related information and provide a open platform for general public to discuss or ask for help.

We are so welcome any member to ask and share update information, and we also provide profession opinions to people in need.

We hope what we do can people with need, stop them in the first place.
